Liziqi is a food and country life blogger who gained fame for her unique approach of filming. Li first started posting her videos on a video sharing app called Meipai in 2015. Initially, Li made her videos all by herself. She expressed her amateurish video editing skills failed to "capture the creativity". In 2016, one of Li's videos titled Peach Wine caught the attention of a video-making platform CEO, who featured her video on the platform's front page. This soon elicited more followers on Li's channel. As of October 2020, she has 13 million subscribers on YouTube, over 26 million followers on Sina Weibo, over 3.8 million followers on Facebook, and has inspired many bloggers to post similar content.

Her mainland Chinese audience includes urban millennials. Li's popularity may be attributed to fugu (复古), which is a growing appreciation in modern China for traditional culture. In September 2019, Li stated in an interview with Goldthread, "I simply want people in the city to know where their food comes from."
